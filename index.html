About me

Hello! I am Suho, the developer of the game. I am a Korean high school student studying in Vietnam. As my hobby, I have been developing games on my own for a few years and always dreamed of publishing them. I hope to continue building my interest in other areas in Computer Science to be impactful to people in need.

Personal Objectives/Goal

Using the games I have made for years, I have showcased the video games at the school fair as a fundraising activity, which was very successful and meaningful. Thus, I decided to make games that are dedicated to a charity. I was inspired by the previous fundraising activities held in Blue Dragon that had interactions with children such as auctions for children's painting. To tailor the game to Blue Dragon's needs, I have discussed the game ideas and design with the head of Blue Dragon, constantly exchanging feedback.

Project Overview

Welcome to Blue Dragon Learning Adventure, a game powered by reinforcement learning, crafted for children learning basic English. It combines entertainment and personalized education for an immersive experience in language development.

Objectives

Tailored for young learners, the game integrates user-friendly features and reinforcement learning, offering profile management, sound control, and achievement tracking. Diverse game modes like Hangman, Dodge & Collect, and Guess the Word aim to dynamically boost cognitive skills and vocabulary development.

Embark on your Blue Dragon adventure! Sign in or register to unlock the realms of fun. Customize your profile, manage progress, and explore the game features at your fingertips.

Game Mode 1: Hangman

Challenge your wit with Hangman mode! Guess the hidden words to save the day. Every correct guess earns you rewards, but beware, each wrong move inches the hangman closer. Can you outsmart the challenges and rise to new levels?

Game Mode 2: Dodge & Collect

Dodge & Collect mode awaits! Navigate through falling letters, grab the correct ones to complete the word. Watch out! Lose your hearts with wrong catches. How far can you go before time runs out? Collect coins and experience to prove your skill.

Game Mode 3: Guess the Word

Unleash your linguistic prowess in Guess the Word mode! Decode hidden words, earn rewards for each correct guess. How long can you keep the streak alive? Track your progress, and when you're ready, return to the home screen with your earned spoils.
